---

## **Key Insights**
1. **Geographic Distribution**
   - **United States** leads with **36.9% of all content** (3,690 titles).
   - Netflix is available in **122 countries**, with **Asia (2.57K) and Europe (2.31K)** as major markets.

2. **Content Type Breakdown**
   - **TV Shows (68%)** dominate over **Movies (32%)**.
   - Top genres: **International, Dramas, Comedies**.

3. **Movie Duration Trends**
   - Average movie duration has **increased from 81 min (2008) to 103 min (2021)**.
